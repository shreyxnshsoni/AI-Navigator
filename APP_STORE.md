## Permissions Explanation

**Why we need these permissions:**

- **Read and change data on websites you visit**
  - Required to add the minimap and navigation features to AI chat pages
  - Only processes data locally in your browser
  - Never sends data to external servers

- **Storage**
  - Stores your pins, sessions, and preferences locally
  - Uses browser's local storage / IndexedDB
  - You can clear all data anytime

**We do NOT:**
- Read your conversations on servers
- Store your conversations remotely
- Send your data anywhere
- Track your browsing outside AI chat pages
